1.6 Describing access issues that need taking into account

There are several ways in which you can make your website more disability friendly, these include:

  • Alt tags - the addition of effective alt tags enable screen reading
  • Subtitles - include subtitles on videos
  • Utilise colour control - avoid garish colours and be wary of using yellow, blue and green close to each other
  • Give clickable items a larger button so they are easier to use for mobility problems [5]
