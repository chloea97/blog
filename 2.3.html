2.3 Assess the strengths and weaknesses of your final project

Throughout the build of the website there were a number of obstacles to building the exact design desired. Some of these were overcome through research, including:

  • Use of columns
  • Width and length of images
Others were more complex and I did not have time to execute them properly:
  • Making the website mobile friendly
  • Effective spacing ‘< br >’ was used as a substitute
  • Aligning text underneath a picture

Overall given the time constraints and the level of improvement from no knowledge I am happy with my website. I look forward to broadening my knowledge and implementing new pieces of code into my work.
