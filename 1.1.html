1.1 Describing the purpose of IT in modern business

The requirement for technology in modern business is enormous. It has the ability to streamline, improve or even create many processes which can enable a business to reach its goals whether they include sales, awareness, recruitment etc. Some key tools business can use within technology include;

  • Websites
  • Social Media
  • Organisational and process tools (Accounting software, Sales funnel tracking, Project Management)
  • Security systems
