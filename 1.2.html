1.2 Describing methods, skills and resources needed to complete digital projects successfully

Successful digital projects require a high level of organisation and a keen eye for detail. It is important to create a process to follow at the start of every digital project and make decisions about what methods you will use, what skills you might need and what resources you will need to complete the project. These may include but are not limited to;

Methods

  • Teamwork
  • Planning
  • Brainstorming

Skills

  • Patience
  • Adaptability
  • Eye for detail
  • Creativity

Resources

  • What programmes are needed for coding
  • What programmes are needed for design
  • What skills might you need to outsource
