2.4 Describe further improvements you can make to your project

Further improvements to my project would include adding functionality such as mobile friendliness and alignment of all of the elements on the pages. I would also include a standardised header and footer to the page that sat inside differently formatted boxes as is typical of a normal website.

Other improvements could include an element of interactivity on the website such as when you hover over the pictures, the title appears on a transparent background. Further to this, I would have the recommended blog posts on the home page to alternate.
