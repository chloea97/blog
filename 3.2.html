3.2 Describe ways to improve productivity and efficiency

Productivity and efficiency are important within any project. It is therefore important to reflect on how to improve them before during and after any digital project.

Productivity and efficiency can be affected in a number of ways. These can include; distractions, effectiveness of IT tools, level of planning and level of knowledge. Whilst some of the aspects are controllable such as level of planning, it can be hard within a project to predict obstacles that have arisen through a lack of knowledge, it is also hard to determine how quickly you can pass these obstacles.

In order to improve productivity and efficiency it is important to formulate an effective plan and factor in time for variables that may arise.
